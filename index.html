Portfolio VaR & Risk Metrics

This tool allows you to build a portfolio (up to 5 securities) and calculate Value at Risk (VaR) using different methods:

  • Normal VaR – Assumes returns follow a normal distribution.
  • Historical VaR – Based on actual past market returns.
  • Monte Carlo VaR – Uses simulations for risk modeling.
  • Cornish-Fisher VaR – Adjusts for skewness and kurtosis.

Note: VaR calculations rely on historical price data. If data is missing, some results may not be available.
