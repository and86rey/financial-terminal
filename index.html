Portfolio Risk Analyzer

This tool allows you to build a simple portfolio (up to 5 securities) and analyze its risk exposure using Value at Risk (VaR). You can add stocks by entering their ticker, ISIN, or company name, allocate portfolio weights, and calculate risk metrics at different confidence levels.

⚠️ This service is built on limited free licenses for demo purposes only. It leverages the FRM API, which has limited securities coverage. Performance and data availability may be restricted.
